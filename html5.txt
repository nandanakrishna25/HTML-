
1.<article>
  To specify a block of content.
  Used for blogs or articles.
  <div>
    
2.<dialog>
  An interactive component or a dialog box.
  Used for pop up alerts,subwindow.
  <div> or <span>
  
3.<figure>       
  To illustrate figure or image related to the content displayed.
  <img> or <div>

4.<figcaption>       
  Defines a caption for a figure.
  <p>

5.<footer>
  Contains content that is to be displayed at the very end of the page.
  <div>

6.<header>      
  Contains title and heading info related to the page.
  <div>

7.<hgroup>
  A group of headings.
  <div>

8.<main>
  Groups the main or important content of a page.
  <div>
    
9.<mark>
  To highlight text.
  <span>
    
10.<nav>
   Navigation bar of a page.
   <ul>

11.<section>
   Groups a section of related content in a page.
   <div>  

12.<meter>
   Scalar measurement with min and max values.
   <progress>
   min,max,low,high,,value,optimum

13.<aside>
   Contains related content 
   Side panels
   Article related content
   <div>

14.<details>
   Creates a drop down 
   Contains extra information which user can click and obtain if needed
   Interactive
   open

15.<summary>
   Used in details tag 
   Title of summary

16.<output>
   Result of an operation
   for,name,form

17.<time>
   Indicated time and date
   datetime
